### Human Verification Required

**1. Install form Places autocomplete widget (browser)**

**Test:** Open `/slack/setup?team_id=<real_team>` in a browser with a real `GOOGLE_PLACES_API_KEY` configured. Type at least 3 characters of a known office address in the address field. Wait ~300 ms.
**Expected:** A dropdown of suggestions appears sourced from `/places/autocomplete`. Click a suggestion — the hidden `place_id` field is populated and the "Save & find restaurants" button becomes enabled. Submit the form. A `workspace_locations` row is created, a background restaurant seed runs, and the success page is displayed.
**Why human:** Inline JS debounce, DOM manipulation, and the browser-to-/places/autocomplete fetch loop cannot be exercised via pytest.

**2. /lunch always-prompt in unbound channel (live Slack)**

**Test:** In a Slack channel that has no `channel_locations` binding, invoke `/lunch`. Observe the ephemeral response. Pick an existing office from the dropdown. Invoke `/lunch` a second time.
**Expected:** First invocation shows the prompt with both the static_select and the "Add a new office" button — even if the workspace has only one office. Second invocation posts the lunch poll without showing the prompt again (binding was saved silently).
**Why human:** Slack interactive message rendering, button rendering, and ephemeral delivery cannot be verified without a live Slack integration.

**3. Add-office modal from /lunch prompt (live Slack)**

**Test:** In an unbound channel, click "Add a new office" on the always-prompt. Type at least 3 characters in the modal's address field (external_select). Pick a prediction. Submit the modal.
**Expected:** A new `workspace_locations` row is created, the channel is bound to it, and a lunch poll is immediately posted in the channel. The structlog `office_create` event with `via=lunch_prompt` appears in logs.
**Why human:** Slack modal delivery, external_select interaction, and the view_submission webhook require a live Slack integration.

**4. App Home Offices section rendering and admin gate (live Slack)**

**Test:** Open LunchBot's App Home as (a) a workspace admin and (b) a regular member. For the admin view, click Rename on an office and save. Click "Set default" on a non-default office. Click Delete and confirm.
**Expected:** Admin sees per-row Rename/Set-default/Delete buttons; non-admin sees only the always-available "Add office" button. After rename the row reflects the new name. After set-default the star badge moves. After delete the row disappears and bound channels prompt on next /lunch.
**Why human:** App Home publish events, users.info admin check, and Block Kit rendering require a live Slack workspace with a valid bot token.

**5. Office delete cascade observed from Slack (live Slack)**

**Test:** Bind at least one channel to an office via /lunch. Then delete that office from App Home (as admin). Invoke /lunch in the previously bound channel.
**Expected:** The channel now shows the always-prompt again, confirming the cascade-delete unbound it.
**Why human:** Requires live Slack to confirm the channel reverts to the prompted state; DB inspection needed to confirm `channel_locations` row removal.
